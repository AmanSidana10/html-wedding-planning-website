Delicious Wedding Menu Suggestions

A wedding menu is one of the most memorable aspects of your big day. Here are some of our choice to help you craft a menu that caters to all your guests, or you can just choose us :)

1.Appetizers

  • Vegetable Spring Rolls
  • Mini Samosas
  • Cheese and Olive Skewers
  • Paneer Tikka
  • Chicken or Fish Tikka

2.Main Course

  • Vegetarian Options:
    • Paneer Butter Masala
    • Dal Makhani
    • Mixed Vegetable Curry
    • Jeera Rice or Pulao
  • Non-Vegetarian Options:
    • Butter Chicken
    • Mutton Rogan Josh
    • Fish Curry
    • Biryani (Chicken or Mutton)
  • Breads:
    • Naan (Plain, Garlic, Butter)
    • Roti
    • Paratha

3.Desserts

  • Gulab Jamun
  • Rasmalai
  • Jalebi with Rabri
  • Fruit Custard
  • Chocolate Mousse

4.Beverages

  • Mocktails (Virgin Mojito, Fruit Punch)
  • Fresh Fruit Juices
  • Chai (Masala Tea)
  • Traditional Drinks (Lassi, Aam Panna)

Tips for Crafting Your Wedding Menu

  • Include a mix of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options to cater to all dietary preferences.
  • Offer regional specialties to add a personal touch to your menu.
  • Ensure there are allergy-friendly options (e.g., gluten-free, nut-free).
  • Plan for seasonal dishes to ensure fresh ingredients.
