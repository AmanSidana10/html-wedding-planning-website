Essential Tips for Your Dream Wedding

  • Set Your Budget Early: Determine your overall budget and allocate it to major categories to stay on track financially.
  • Create a Wedding Timeline: Plan key milestones like booking vendors and sending invitations well in advance.
  • Choose Your Theme: Decide on a theme that reflects your style, such as rustic, modern, or vintage.
  • Book Your Venue First: Secure a venue that matches your guest list size and theme as soon as possible.
  • Keep Your Guest List Manageable: Prioritize close family and friends to stay within budget and venue capacity.
  • Hire Reliable Vendors: Research and book reputable vendors with good reviews and recommendations.
  • Send Invitations Early: Give your guests ample time to RSVP and make travel arrangements.
  • Plan for Guest Comfort: Ensure your guests have transportation, accommodations, and weather-appropriate attire suggestions.
  • Schedule a Trial Run: Test hair, makeup, and catering options in advance to avoid surprises.
  • Enjoy the Process: Remember to take breaks and celebrate small milestones during planning.
