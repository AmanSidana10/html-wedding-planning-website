Popular Wedding Music Genres

Explore some of the most popular music genres for different parts of your wedding day!

Classical Music

Elegant and timeless, classical music is perfect for your ceremony and reception.

  • Wedding March – Mendelssohn
  • Canon in D – Pachelbel
  • Air on the G String – Bach

Jazz Music

Set a sophisticated and lively atmosphere with smooth jazz for your cocktail hour and reception.

  • At Last – Etta James
  • What A Wonderful World – Louis Armstrong
  • Fly Me to the Moon – Frank Sinatra

Pop Music

For a fun and lively reception, pop music is always a crowd-pleaser.

  • Perfect – Ed Sheeran
  • Happy – Pharrell Williams
  • Marry You – Bruno Mars

Indie Music

Indie music brings a modern, unique touch to your wedding day.

  • First Day of My Life – Bright Eyes
  • Home – Edward Sharpe & The Magnetic Zeros
  • Everything – Michael Bublé

Wedding Music Tips

Choosing the right music can set the perfect tone for your wedding. Here are some tips:

  • Consider your venue: Some venues have acoustics that suit certain types of music better.
  • Choose music that reflects your personalities as a couple.
  • Don't forget to include your guests: Mix different genres to keep everyone happy.
  • Work with a professional DJ or band to ensure smooth transitions between songs.
  • Choose songs for each section of your wedding: ceremony, cocktail hour, dinner, and reception.
